Analysis of Preincarnate Souls and Their Effects Upon Birth

    She is... special.

    The project was, beyond any doubt, a success. The birth process was successfully accomplished, and she was "born" without defect.

    There was one... difficulty. Every twelve hours, she changes. Her form is completely replaced, alternating between two differing appearances. They seem to have bearing on her physical attributes too- she gains and loses weight, height- evel full limbs!

    I guess that's what we get for going this route. I said I was okay to have a child the old-fashioned way, but she wanted to try this, and that's alright. Next time, if we do this again, we're working with only one of our genes.

    I love her, and I love Adella.
