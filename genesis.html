A Complete History of the Scholarly Council

  • November 15, 1420 || ARPPRXZ and INPORI'C CGJTF begin an assault on UOHYR MJOIR. There are no mortal survivors, and the city was destroyed.
  • November 17, 1420 || HJBRZ RZGRGOC is constructed over the course of 24 hours, serving as a replacement to UOHYR MJOIR.
  • November 25, 1420 || ARPPRXZ selects five wizards from the town of CSTJHX, forming the first Scholarly Council.
  • January 10, 1421 || The Scholarly Council organizes a public forum in HJBRZ RZGRGOC to address concerns and inquiries from the citizens about the recent assault and the city's reconstruction.
  • April 8, 1421 || The Scholarly Council convenes to establish a magical barrier around HJBRZ RZGRGOC to protect it from potential threats.
  • March 5, 1422 || ARPPRXZ is reunited with RTSLJH.
  • October 20, 1423 || Matter is mysteriously removed from the universe.
  • October 22, 1423 || The Scholarly Council becomes aware of (and successfully contains) anomalous elements outside of HJBRZ RZGRGOC.
  • October 30, 1423 || The Scholarly Council discovers the origins of anomalous elements. In the process, information on the first-discovered alternative universe ("UNIVERSE X" in some documents) is uncovered.
  • November 2, 1423 || Several unsuccessful attempts to return anomalous elements to UNIVERSE X occur. A human female named "POR" is discovered to have been the matter originally removed from the universe; this leads to the hypothesis that matter must be exchanged between two universes.
  • December 31, 1451 || RXZPPR is born to ARPPRXZ and RTSLJH through manipulation of Energy Planes and Pre-Incarnate Souls.
  • September 12, 1468 || RXZPPR, sanctioned by the Scholarly Council, begins an expedition into deep space.
  • September 18, 1468 || RXZPPR receives a vision of her own death. The psychological damage caused by this vision persists through the rest of her life.
    Additional Information - The vision, when asked, contained three key elements:
     1. An individual with a large firearm was seen shouting at their companion.
     2. The individual was short and male. It's unclear if he was Dwarven in descent, but RXZPPR believes that to be the case.
     3. The indiviual pointed the gun at her and fired.
  • February 3, 1471 || RXZPPR confronts an individual whom she suspects to be the person seen in her vision.
    The individual is not the assumed person, but fires a handgun out of self-defense. RXZPPR receives a lethal gunshot wound.
  • February 15, 1471 || RXZPPR confronts an individual whom she suspects to be the person seen in her vision.
    The individual is the assumed person but was not hostile towards RXZPPR. After a long confrontation, his companion realizes RXZPPR's lineage and believes her to contain divinity. The individual is ordered to shoot RXZPPR and complies.
  • April 2, 1471 || RXZPPR, suffering from intense paranoia from her vision, takes residency in a small home. She does not leave that house for the remainder of her life.
  • December 10, 1473 || Fearing for her death, RXZPPR begins refusing to eat for fear of fatal poison.
  • December 31, 1473 || RXZPPR, on her 22nd birthday, enters a coma from lack of nutrients.
